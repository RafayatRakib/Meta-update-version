OUR SERVICES

OCEAN FREIGHT SHIPPING

Whether you have full container load (FCL), less than container load (LCL) or non-containerized load, we have the sea containers you need for your sea freight shipping requirements.

We are the go-to export and import partner for competitive rates, frequent departures and the capacity you need – to and from every major port in the world. Whether your needs are complex or straightforward and whatever your goods, we can help you with you shipping needs as well as other deliveries by air, road, or rail.

We have employees with detailed knowledge of local and international export and import compliance guidelines. Our staff can assist you in understanding each country’s requirements, as well as local pickup and last mile delivery to ensure your shipment is hassle-free.

AIR FREIGHT SHIPPING

Our air freight service comprises air cargo and air courier all at unbeatable prices for our clients. Our air freight services are crafted to ensure we ship your goods faster across the world with air cargo services. Clients can enjoy reduced damage risk and shorter lead time with our air shipping options that provide a sense of security for customers who send goods that require certain special handling processes.

Air courier shipment ensures your goods arrive in record time. Our companies’ flexibility and guaranteed capacity for your high-value air cargo guarantees our ability to effortlessly move your high-value goods at short notice to anywhere in the world.

Our air freight service provides customized solutions and guarantees global reach and vast capacity – 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. We manage aircraft cargo on specific routes, but also to structure and oversee processes on the ground. This ensures the best possible control, security and efficiency from door to door for your priority air shipping.

CAR SHIPPING

We confidently and professionally handle single shipment or multiple full loads weekly, daily or as frequently as you need it. We can handle your cargo with utmost care and give you the best solution for your road freight needs.

We make sure your cargo is delivered safely and efficiently wherever it needs to go. And all our delivery and freight personnel are certified pros. Our experienced freight forwarders and sales staff know your market and local transport conditions. Whether you need one batch transported or multiple shipments each day, we can make sure you get the transport solution that matches your needs exactly.
